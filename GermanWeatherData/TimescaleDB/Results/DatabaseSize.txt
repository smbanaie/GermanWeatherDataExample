```
SELECT pg_database.datname as "database_name", pg_size_pretty(pg_database_size(pg_database.datname)) AS size_in_mb
FROM pg_database 
WHERE pg_database.datname like 'sampledb'
ORDER by size_in_mb DESC;
```

database_name  size_in_mb
-------------- --------------------
